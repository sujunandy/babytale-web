Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple. To cancel: open the iPhone Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Babytale → Cancel Subscription. Your Premium features stay active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I share my baby with my partner?

Settings → tap the baby's row → "Share baby". You'll get a code. Your partner taps "Have a code" on their own Settings screen and enters it. Both phones will sync the same baby in real time. Sharing is free.

Why is my tracker not syncing to my partner's phone?

Tracker sync requires Premium on the device that owns the baby (the original creator). The co-parent doesn't need to be Premium to view or edit the synced data. If you're on Premium and sync still isn't working, force-quit the app on both phones and reopen — the sync channel will rebuild on first load.

I submitted a recipe — when will it appear?

Every submission is reviewed by a human moderator before it goes live, usually within 24 hours. You'll see it in the "Yours" filter with a "Pending review" badge while you wait. Once approved, it appears in the global community feed and the badge disappears.

Can I delete a recipe I submitted to the community?

Yes. Open the recipe and tap the menu (•••) → "Remove from community". Your private copy stays in Yours.

How do I change my display name?

Currently the display name shown on community recipes is the one you used at first submission. Email us if you'd like it changed — we'll update it manually until we ship a self-service editor.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → "Delete account" at the bottom. This wipes your data from our servers immediately and signs you out. Local data on the device is also wiped, returning the app to its first-launch state. This action is permanent.

Does Babytale work offline?

Yes, most of it. Tracking, browsing bundled recipes, comparing cultures, and viewing memories all work fully offline. Community features (browsing other parents' recipes, commenting, liking) and cloud sync need a connection.

What languages do you support?

English, French, Spanish, German, and Italian. The app picks up your iPhone's language automatically. You can override it from Settings → Language.

Is the recipe information medical advice?

No. Recipes and cultural feeding information in Babytale are starting points, not prescriptions. Every baby is different — please talk to your pediatrician about your baby's specific feeding plan, allergens, and developmental milestones.
