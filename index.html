What's inside

A baby tracker that finally feels personal.

Every feature is built around the way parents actually use these apps — quick taps, soft sounds off, and information that respects your time.

🍼

Smart tracker

Tap once to log a feed, diaper, or nap. Breast and bottle timers run live on the lock screen and Dynamic Island so you can stop them without unlocking. Smart defaults remember your last bottle amount and alternate the breast side automatically.

🌍

350+ recipes from 62 countries

Bundled first foods from Japanese okayu to Italian pastina — all stage-tagged and filterable by age, country, or what your baby is ready for next. The Home tab rotates a daily featured recipe biased toward your family's cultures.

📖

Cultural compare

See how Japanese and French families feed at the same age. Cultural averages from public health and ethnographic sources, not Instagram.

🍽️

Community recipes

Share a recipe from your own kitchen, or browse what parents in other countries are feeding their babies right now. Every submission runs through three safety layers before it lands in the feed — deterministic rule checks, AI-assisted review, and community reports. How it works →

📲

Home-screen widgets

Quick Log tiles for one-tap feed, diaper, and breast-timer start — without opening the app. A Today summary widget shows your counts at a glance. Add via long-press → Edit Home Screen → search Babylore.

📝

Visit summary

One-tap PDF summary of the last 7 days — feeds, sleep, diapers, and today's log. Email it to your doctor before the appointment.

👪

Share with a co-parent

Send your partner a code to share the same baby. Both phones log to the same timeline in real time, no subscription needed.

🔐

Privacy-first

Sign in with Apple — we never see your password, and Hide My Email works if you don't want to share an address. No ads, no analytics, no data brokers. Export your tracker data anytime, or request a full account dump under GDPR.

Safer for babies

Every community recipe runs through three safety layers.

Baby food is the one category where bad user-generated content can hurt a child. So every recipe submitted to the community feed goes through a layered safety review before another parent ever sees it.

✅

Rule checks

Honey under 12 months, raw fish under 24 months, whole nuts under 36 months, and other documented hazards block submissions automatically. No human needed.

🧠

AI safety review

A pediatric-nutrition prompt running on Anthropic's Claude API catches subtler concerns — choking textures, untagged allergens, age-inappropriate prep — before publish.

🚩

Community reports

Any signed-in parent can flag a recipe. After three unresolved reports, the recipe is automatically held for review.
